Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

Rivvix collects data to provide and improve our services. This may include your Discord ID, server information, and interactions with the bot. We do not collect personal information unless necessary for our services.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to ensure the proper functioning of Rivvix, including managing commands, logging events, and improving user experience. We do not use your data for any unauthorized purposes.

3. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access and disclosure. However, please be aware that no system can be completely secure.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties except as necessary to provide and improve our services or as required by law.

5. User Rights

You have the right to request access to your data, request corrections, and ask for deletion of your data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@rivvix.com.

6. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page, and your continued use of Rivvix signifies your acceptance of these changes.

7. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@rivvix.com.
